 Pretty pet dogs

I longed for a pet since I was in Class 2. I loved pets a lot (I still do), and back then I used to tell my parents, please get me a pet dog, and I will love him more than i love you guys. But my mom did not allow as she had a sad experience of being very sad and morose when your pet passes away. She had been through that in her childhood and always told us brothers, that get your own pet when you grow up.

Although my mom never paid heed to our wishes (I and my brother) to get a pet, but she had insane faith in astrology. Once an astrologer told her to own a black colored pet dog and upon doing so everything would be hale and hearty. Voila! Enters my life, the love of my life, Tipsy. I fell in love with him the moment I saw him. He was a dashed (the ones who resemble hot dog). Born on a very auspicious date 03/03/2003.

It just took 2–3 weeks for Tipsy to become integral part of our nuclear family’s life. Apart from I and my brother, Mom, dad, our helpers, everyone could not imagine their lives without Tipsy. He was not a dog. I used to feel he is an emotional human being.

His eyes and barking used to do all the talking. It was as if we understood his language. He used to bark at us angrily and endlessly when we came back from week long holidays after leaving him alone at home. He made it a point to go individually in everyone’s room and bark at that guy in person, and scold him for 5 minutes. He used to go and sit on the main gate daily, at 6pm, unfailingly because it used to be the time of papa coming back from office. Never he used to be a minute early or late
